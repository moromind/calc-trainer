1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Calc Trainer ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mental arithmetic training application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Calc Trainer is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect or process personal data such as:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Personal identifying information
  • Contact information (unless voluntarily provided for support)
  • Location data
  • User-generated content
  • Account credentials (no accounts required)

3. Telemetry and Analytics

We use anonymous telemetry tools (such as Microsoft Application Insights) to help us understand how users interact with the application and to monitor performance, usage, and errors.

3.1 Data Collected Automatically

The following types of non-personal data may be collected automatically:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Screen views and user navigation patterns
  • Error reports and performance metrics
  • An anonymized user and session identifier

3.2 Purpose of Data Collection

We do not collect or process personal data such as name, email address, phone number, or any other information that directly identifies the user.

All collected telemetry data is used exclusively for diagnostic and improvement purposes. This information is not shared with any third parties and is stored securely in accordance with applicable data protection regulations.

3.3 IP Address Handling

IP addresses are anonymized automatically before storage or processing.

4. Local Data Storage

User progress and performance data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Your last 10 results for each operation type
  • Performance statistics and accuracy rates
  • App settings and preferences
  • Training difficulty levels and progress

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers except for the anonymous analytics mentioned above.

5. No User Accounts Required

Calc Trainer does not require user registration or account creation. You can use the App immediately upon installation without providing any personal information.

6. Communications

Calc Trainer does not send:

  • SMS messages
  • Push notifications
  • Email communications (unless you contact us for support)
  • Marketing materials

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses Microsoft Azure Application Insights for anonymous analytics. This service operates under Microsoft's privacy policies and security standards. No personal data is shared with this or any other third-party service.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the limited data we collect:

  • All analytics data is anonymized before transmission
  • Data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
  • Access to analytics data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security assessments and updates are performed

9. Children's Privacy

Calc Trainer is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or any users. The App is designed to function without requiring any personal data input.

10. Your Rights

Even though we don't collect personal data, you have the right to:

  • Contact us for information about our data practices
  • Request information about any data associated with your session identifier
  • Request removal of any data associated with your session identifier
  • Uninstall the App at any time to stop all data collection

10.1 Contact for Data Requests

Users may contact us at countymcdigits@gmail.com to request more information or to have any data associated with their session identifier removed, even if it does not contain personal information.

11. International Data Transfers

Anonymous analytics data may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States (through Microsoft Azure). All transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and are subject to appropriate safeguards.

12. Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained for operational purposes and app improvement. Since this data is not personally identifiable, standard data retention periods apply based on technical and business requirements.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the App and on our website. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

14. App Store Privacy Policies

Your use of the App is also subject to the privacy policies of the app store from which you downloaded it (Google Play Store or Apple App Store).

15. Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with applicable privacy laws, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Polish Personal Data Protection Act
  • Other applicable regional privacy laws
