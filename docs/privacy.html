1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Calc Trainer ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mental arithmetic training application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Calc Trainer is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect or process personal data such as:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Personal identifying information
  • Contact information (unless voluntarily provided for support)
  • Location data
  • User-generated content
  • Account credentials (no accounts required)

3. Telemetry and Analytics

We use anonymous telemetry tools (such as Microsoft Application Insights) to help us understand how users interact with the application and to monitor performance, usage, and errors.

3.1 Data Collected Automatically

The following types of non-personal data may be collected automatically:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Screen views and user navigation patterns
  • Error reports and performance metrics
  • An anonymized user and session identifier

3.2 Purpose of Data Collection

We do not collect or process personal data such as name, email address, phone number, or any other information that directly identifies the user.

All collected telemetry data is used exclusively for diagnostic and improvement purposes. This information is not shared with any third parties and is stored securely in accordance with applicable data protection regulations.

3.3 IP Address Handling

IP addresses are anonymized automatically before storage or processing.

4. Website Analytics and Cookies

Our website (landing page) uses Google Analytics and cookies to understand how visitors interact with our site and to improve the user experience.

4.1 Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality, including cookie consent preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics cookies to understand website usage patterns (only loaded with your consent)

4.2 Google Analytics

With your consent, we use Google Analytics to collect anonymous information about how visitors use our website, including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • How visitors arrive at our website
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device and browser information
  • Website interaction patterns

Google Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated. We do not use it to identify individual users.

4.3 Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can:

  • Accept All: Allow essential and analytics cookies
  • Reject All: Only essential cookies will be used (no Google Analytics)

Your choice is stored for one year. You can change your preferences by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting our website.

4.4 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Clear all cookies when closing the browser

5. Local Data Storage

User progress and performance data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Your last 10 results for each operation type
  • Performance statistics and accuracy rates
  • App settings and preferences
  • Training difficulty levels and progress

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers except for the anonymous analytics mentioned above.

6. No User Accounts Required

Calc Trainer does not require user registration or account creation. You can use the App immediately upon installation without providing any personal information.

7. Communications

Calc Trainer does not send:

  • SMS messages
  • Push notifications
  • Email communications (unless you contact us for support)
  • Marketing materials

8. Third-Party Services

The App uses Microsoft Azure Application Insights for anonymous analytics. Our website uses Google Analytics (with your consent). These services operate under their respective privacy policies and security standards. No personal data is shared with these or any other third-party services.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the limited data we collect:

  • All analytics data is anonymized before transmission
  • Data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
  • Access to analytics data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security assessments and updates are performed
